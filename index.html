My Why

Coding wasn't something I was always attracted to, it only came to my attention once I started working in a tech company and felt way out of my league. I had thought of doing a coding bootcamp earlier but it never seemed like it was the right time. There was too much going on at our startup to justify the away and as I was always on the road it was not possible to attend in person. So, once covid-19 hit and the world came to a halt, it was now or never! I started with codecademy, on my own, to see if I could get the hang of things, see if it was something I could actually, turns out, I was! With my excitement at an all time high I started to once again look at the bootcamps available in town and who would have a class starting soon. After some research and talking to my teammates they all agreed that the curriculum at Le Wagon would be a great start so without hesitation I applied!

My Expectations

Take a sabbatical from work, code during the day and enjoy my evenings and come out of this nine weeks later feeling like I've accomplished something amazing and feel empowered. I knew I would have some difficulties because of my ADHD and lack of concentration but I have a therapist and we've worked on ways to manage these, so should be fine. Even before the first day of class there's about 60+ hrs of prep work so things should be breezy! We learned all the concepts and now we are just going to reinforce what we've learned, right!?
